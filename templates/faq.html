1. How to download ?
After authorization, an additional download button appears on the work's page when you open the volume in the book's table of contents.
2. I'm logged in, but the download is still not available. What's wrong ?
For a small part of the content, downloading may be disabled. For such content, the *Download option is enabled for this content. If the material is closed due to its popularity - downloading will be enabled in a few months after the translation is completed. But downloading may also be closed for technical reasons.
3. The chapter has incorrect formatting/text problems, what to do ?
On the chapter page, under the text, there is a "complaint" form to report problems.
4. What is the policy of the site? Do you "leak" paid chapters from other resources?
Not by hand. If the content is freely available (anyone can find it in a couple of minutes), we think there is no point in hiding anything. That's why we can post chapters that are closed on the translator's resource, but have been online for a long time. We do not buy chapters for purposeful posting on our site. We always try to specify the original source of translation (if it is not specified - write to us) and all translators. If you want content to appear on our site with a delay - write to us. If you own the official rights to translate and distribute the book - write to us.
5. You don't have any content at all that the translators put out directly?
Recently, translators have been posted directly. But these are isolated cases when we have been approached by mail on this issue. For now, we are still not a fully open platform.
